About

Physical fitness is not only one of the most important keys to a healthy body, it is the basis of dynamic and creative intellectual activity.

Keeping the above statement in mind 'Bread to Shred' aims at pushing people to their limit, hence leading a healthy and fit life. As discussed below the services offered are a calorie counter, a forum, blogs by various dietitians and health experts and a gallery which benefits all the users.

Our Services

1

We offer a calorie counter as a function. One can set a goal and depending on the calories gained through food or calories burnt through exercise, the goal-setter can update his/her calorie count.

2

Blogs of various dietitians and health experts are also available to guide the users. These blogs are extremely helpful for all kinds of people.

3

The gallery shares images of some good diet plans, food items as well as good exercises. Exercise and health related videos can also be found in the gallery.
